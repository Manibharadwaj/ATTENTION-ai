About Study Guard

Revolutionizing productivity through AI-powered focus monitoring

Our Mission

Study Guard was born from the need to help students and professionals maintain focus during long work sessions. We believe that technology should enhance human productivity, not distract from it.

Using cutting-edge AI and computer vision, we've created a tool that monitors attention patterns and provides gentle reminders to stay on track, helping users build better study habits and achieve their goals.

How It Works

Our AI analyzes facial features and eye movements in real-time to detect attention levels:

  • Eye tracking for sleep detection
  • Face orientation monitoring
  • Gaze direction analysis
  • Focus score calculation
  • Smart alarm system

Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies and AI frameworks:

Python Flask
MediaPipe AI
OpenCV
Socket.IO
JavaScript
HTML5/CSS3

Privacy First

Your privacy is our top priority. All face tracking and analysis happens locally on your device - no data is sent to external servers or stored anywhere.

The AI processing uses MediaPipe's lightweight models that run efficiently on standard hardware, ensuring your personal information stays completely private.
